Privacy Policy

At Food City, we are committed to safeguarding the privacy of our customers and ensuring that your personal information is protected. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to protect your data when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information from you when you visit our website or make purchases:

  • Personal Information: Such as your name, email address, phone number, postal address, and payment information when you make an online purchase or create an account.
  • Transactional Information: Details of the products you purchase, delivery preferences, and payment methods.
  • Technical Data: Information about your device, browser type, IP address, and browsing activity collected through cookies and similar tracking technologies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To process your orders and deliver the products you purchase.
  • To communicate with you about your orders, including confirmation and delivery updates.
  • To send you promotional offers, newsletters, and updates if you have subscribed to our communications.
  • To improve our website and enhance your shopping experience through analytics and customer feedback.
  • To comply with legal obligations and resolve any disputes.

3. Sharing Your Information

Food City does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • When necessary to complete your transaction (e.g., sharing your address with a delivery service).
  • To trusted third-party service providers who assist in website hosting, payment processing, and marketing services.
  • To comply with legal requirements or respond to law enforcement requests.

4. Security of Your Information

We take the security of your personal information seriously. We use a variety of security measures, including encryption, firewalls, and secure servers, to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You can manage your account details, including preferences for marketing communications, by logging into your account or contacting our customer support. Additionally, you may request to unsubscribe from our emails at any time.

6. Use of Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your shopping experience, analyze website traffic, and provide personalized content. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but doing so may affect the functionality of our website.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. Any updates will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically.
